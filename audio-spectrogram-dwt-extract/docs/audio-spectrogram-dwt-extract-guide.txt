LAB 6 - TACH ANH TU AUDIO STEGO BANG DWT VA KEY

Muc tieu
-------
Sender co san hai file:

  1. stego.wav  - audio da duoc nhung anh bi mat
  2. secret.key - key va thong tin can thiet de tach anh

Receiver se lam tung cong doan:

  1. Nhan stego.wav va secret.key.
  2. Tach tin hieu bi mat tu he so tan so cao cua DWT.
  3. Dung key de dao hoan vi va dung lai anh.
  4. Mo anh da tach de quan sat.

Sau moi task, co the chay checkwork de xem muc tuong ung chuyen sang Y.

Task 1 - Kiem tra file o sender
-------------------------------
Mo terminal sender:

  cd ~/stego
  ls -l
  cat README_sender.txt
  ./play_stego.sh

Sender phai co:

  stego.wav
  secret.key

Task 2 - Bat SSH tren receiver
------------------------------
Mo terminal receiver:

  sudo service ssh start
  systemctl status ssh

Task 3 - Gui stego.wav va secret.key sang receiver
--------------------------------------------------
Mo terminal sender:

  scp ~/stego/stego.wav ~/stego/secret.key ubuntu@receiver:~/stego/
  ssh ubuntu@receiver "python3 ~/stego/refresh_status.py"

Trong terminal Labtainer:

  checkwork

Muc can Y:
  audio_received
  key_received

Task 4 - Tach tin hieu bi mat tu DWT
------------------------------------
Mo terminal receiver:

  cd ~/stego
  nano extract_signal_task.py

Sua TODO thanh:

  STEGO_FILE = "stego.wav"
  KEY_FILE = "secret.key"

Chay:

  python3 extract_signal_task.py
  checkwork

Ket qua tao ra:
  hidden_signal.json

Muc can Y:
  dwt_signal_extracted

Task 5 - Dung key de khoi phuc anh
----------------------------------
Mo terminal receiver:

  nano recover_image_task.py

Sua TODO thanh:

  HIDDEN_SIGNAL = "hidden_signal.json"
  KEY_FILE = "secret.key"

Chay:

  python3 recover_image_task.py
  checkwork

Ket qua tao ra:
  recovered_secret.png

Muc can Y:
  key_permutation_used
  secret_image_recovered
  recovered_image_valid

Task 6 - Mo anh da tach
-----------------------
Trong receiver:

  ./view_recovered.sh
  checkwork

Muc can Y:
  recovered_image_viewed

Ket qua checkwork cuoi cung
---------------------------
  Y - audio_received
  Y - key_received
  Y - dwt_signal_extracted
  Y - key_permutation_used
  Y - secret_image_recovered
  Y - recovered_image_viewed
  Y - recovered_image_valid

Ket thuc lab:
  stoplab audio-spectrogram-dwt-extract
